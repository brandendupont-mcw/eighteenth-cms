# Diversion and Deferral

This section presents data on cases granted diversion or deferred judgment. Diversion programs and deferred judgments are alternatives to traditional prosecution that attempt to address individualsâ€™ needs. If programmatic requirements are met, the case will be dismissed.

**Why is this important?** Understanding what cases are diverted or deferred and the rate of successful program completion provides information on whether the alternative minimizes unnecessary punitiveness while centering community safety.
